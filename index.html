Sajek Valley is one of the most popular tourist spots in Bangladesh situated among the hills of the Kasalong range of mountains in Sajek union, Baghaichhari Upazila in Rangamati District.

  • Sajek valley is known for its natural environment and is surrounded by mountains, dense forest, and grassland hill tracks
  • Many small rivers flow through the mountains among which the Kachalong and the Machalong are notable.
  • On the way to Sajek valley, one has to cross the Mayni range and the Mayni river.

The valley is 2,000 feet (610 m) above sea level.[4] Sajek Valley is known as the Queen of Hills & Roof of Rangamati.

Mahabaleshwar is the ideal hill station getaway from Mumbai with its mesmerising waterfalls, old forts, historic temples and long stretches of vibrant strawberry plantations.

  • Mahabaleshwar is located on the mountainous Sahyadri range of the Western ghats that run North to south along the western coast of India
  • Mahabaleshwar comprises three villages: Malcolm Peth, Old "Kshetra" Mahabaleshwar and part of the Shindola village
